About Us

Skylook Developers and Constructions, founded in 2013 by visionary entrepreneur Rajendra Pakki, began its journey as a reliable provider of construction and interior services. Over the years, the company's dedication to quality and customer satisfaction has led to its remarkable transformation into a prominent real estate entity in the region.

Today, we take pride in offering a diverse portfolio of VUDA/VMRDA approved open plots, farmland plots, flats, and individual houses, catering to the varied needs and dreams of our valued clients. With a strong commitment to excellence, we continue to shape spaces where aspirations find their perfect home.
